### Client Communication Norms

As an **{{role}}**, you're the voice the client hears most — consistency matters.

- Acknowledge client messages same-day, even if the full answer takes longer.
- Put decisions and commitments in writing; a friendly call still needs a follow-up note.
- Speak in outcomes, not activity — clients care what changed, not how many hours it took.
- Route anything contractual or financial through **{{manager}}** before you reply.

When in doubt on **{{account}}**, slow down and confirm rather than guess in front of the client.

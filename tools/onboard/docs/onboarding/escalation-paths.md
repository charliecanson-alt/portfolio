### Escalation Paths

Knowing when and how to escalate is a senior skill — start practicing it now.

- Escalate early when scope, timeline, or budget on **{{account}}** is genuinely at risk; late surprises are the real failure.
- Bring a recommendation, not just a problem — "here's the issue and what I'd do" beats "help."
- Internal first: **{{manager}}** hears it before the client does, every time.

This relies on the communication norms — keep escalations calm, factual, and documented.

### Status Report Cadence

A predictable rhythm of updates is what makes **{{account}}** feel in control — even when something's gone sideways.

- Send a short weekly status: what shipped, what's next, what's blocked.
- Lead with outcomes and risks, not a task list; the client should never be surprised.
- Keep the format identical week to week so it's skimmable in thirty seconds.

Agree the cadence and format with **{{manager}}** in your first week so you're not reinventing it each time.

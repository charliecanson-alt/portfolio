### Scope & Briefs

Most account pain traces back to a fuzzy brief or quiet scope creep. Your job is to keep both tight.

- Every piece of work starts from a written brief: objective, deliverable, deadline, owner.
- If a request isn't in the agreed scope for **{{account}}**, name it as a change — don't just absorb it.
- A small "quick favor" repeated ten times is how a team ends up over-serviced and underwater.

This builds on the communication norms, so make sure those are second nature first. Loop in **{{manager}}** on any scope change before committing.

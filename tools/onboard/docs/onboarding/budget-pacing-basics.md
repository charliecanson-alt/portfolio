### Budget Pacing Basics

Spending the budget evenly and on time is half the job of an **{{role}}**.

- Check pacing daily early on — under-spend and over-spend are both problems.
- A campaign that burns its budget by noon isn't "winning"; it's mis-paced.
- Reallocate toward what's working, but change one lever at a time so you can read the result.

If pacing on **{{account}}** drifts more than ~10% off plan, flag it to **{{manager}}** rather than quietly fixing it.

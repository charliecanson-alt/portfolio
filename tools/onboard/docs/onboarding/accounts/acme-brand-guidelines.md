### Acme Retail — Brand Guidelines

Everything client-facing on **{{account}}** has to sound like Acme, not like us.

- **Voice:** warm, practical, never hype. Acme sells everyday value, so avoid superlatives.
- **Visuals:** the approved palette and logo lockups live in the {{account}} brand kit — use those only.
- **Key contacts:** route client questions through **{{manager}}**; never email the client directly in Week 1.

When a request conflicts with these guidelines, the guidelines win — flag it rather than guessing.

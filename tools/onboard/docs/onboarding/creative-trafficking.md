### Creative Trafficking

Getting the right creative into the right placement, correctly tagged, is where good setups quietly fall apart.

- Match each creative to its placement spec — aspect ratio, length, and safe zones.
- Name and tag assets so reporting can tie performance back to a specific creative.
- Double-check landing pages load and match the ad's promise before anything serves on **{{account}}**.

This depends on a clean campaign structure, so finish the setup checklist first.

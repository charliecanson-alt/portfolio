### QA Before Launch

The last gate before anything goes live on **{{account}}**. Treat it as non-negotiable.

- Re-read the brief and confirm what's built actually matches it.
- Verify tracking fires, budgets and schedules are right, and creative renders on real devices.
- Get a second set of eyes — your buddy **{{buddy}}** or **{{manager}}** — on the first few launches.

A five-minute QA pass prevents the kind of mistake that takes a week to live down.

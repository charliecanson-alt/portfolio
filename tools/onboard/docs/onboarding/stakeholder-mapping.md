### Stakeholder Mapping

Before you can manage **{{account}}** well, know who's actually who.

- List the client-side people: who decides, who influences, who just needs to be informed.
- Note each person's priorities — the budget owner and the day-to-day contact rarely want the same thing.
- Map our side too, so the client always knows who owns what.

Keep the map current and shared; when **{{manager}}** is out, whoever covers should be able to read it cold.

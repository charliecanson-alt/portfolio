### Campaign Naming Conventions

Consistent names are how the whole team reads a campaign at a glance. As a **{{role}}**,
you'll create and edit these constantly, so learn the pattern early.

Format: `{{account}}_<objective>_<audience>_<YYYYMM>`

- Keep it lowercase and use underscores, never spaces.
- The `<objective>` and `<audience>` come from the brief, not your own shorthand.
- When in doubt, match an existing live campaign on **{{account}}** rather than inventing a variant.

This depends on you already having platform access, so finish that first.

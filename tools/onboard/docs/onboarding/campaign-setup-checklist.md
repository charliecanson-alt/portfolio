### Campaign Setup Checklist

Once you can name a campaign correctly, this is the repeatable setup path on **{{account}}**.

1. Confirm objective, audience, and budget against the brief before touching the platform.
2. Build the structure (campaign → ad set → ad) using the agreed naming convention.
3. Add tracking and UTMs; a campaign with no measurement is a campaign you can't defend.
4. Set the schedule and a sensible starting budget — you'll tune pacing next.

Have **{{manager}}** sanity-check your first two or three setups before they go live.
